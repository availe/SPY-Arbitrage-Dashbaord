# Asset Pricing
Models to determine the fair value of financial assets.
Examples: Black-Scholes model for options pricing, Capital Asset Pricing Model (CAPM).

# Risk Management
Techniques to identify, measure, and mitigate financial risk.
Use of Value at Risk (VaR) and stress testing to assess potential losses.
Portfolio Management
Optimization of investment portfolios using mathematical algorithms.
Concepts: Modern Portfolio Theory (MPT), mean-variance optimization.
Algorithmic Trading
Using automated systems and algorithms to execute trades.
Focus on high-frequency trading, statistical arbitrage, and market-making strategies.
Derivatives and Structured Products
Analysis and pricing of complex financial instruments like options, futures, and swaps.
Hedging strategies to protect against adverse price movements.
